About Me:

My name is Awilda Cantres and I'm originally from the Bronx but currently reside in Brooklyn. Before getting into programming, I wanted to be a performer. That led me to try out different things such as real estate, stage management and playwrighting. Sometimes I wonder if it's also led me to programming. If it did, I'm glad because everyday it grows on me.

On my spare time, I love to create. Whether it's learning a new recipe, constructing a new garment or knitting a scarf, I'm up for it. For me, being able to create something is quite therapeutic.

About Me:

I'm Nuyorican meaning my family comes from Puerto Rico but I was born in New York City. I attended Catholic school 8 years before attending high school where I studied fashion design and garment construction. After that, I studied Communications and Theatre in College. I was then fortunate to be a working actor for some time and even a stage manager for several productions.

Now, I dedicate all my time to coding and hope to give back to others both technically and creatively. When I'm not coding, I'm most likely reading a book, writing, baking or designing something- or at least planning it on Pinterest!

I love the outdoors, animals, and adventures.
